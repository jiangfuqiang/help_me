AosomeBoard 隐私政策

最后更新：2025年1月

概述

AosomeBoard 尊重并保护用户隐私。本应用不收集、不上传、不共享任何用户个人数据。

数据收集

AosomeBoard 不收集任何用户数据，包括但不限于：

  • 个人身份信息
  • 位置信息
  • 使用行为数据
  • 崩溃报告
  • 分析数据

本地存储

应用仅在你的设备本地存储以下信息：

  • 应用设置（通过 UserDefaults 存储）
  • 试用期开始日期
  • 最近打开文件的书签（Security-Scoped Bookmarks）
  • 最近使用的颜色

以上数据仅用于应用自身功能，不会传输到任何服务器。

摄像头权限

应用仅在你主动开启「画中画摄像头」功能时访问摄像头。摄像头画面仅在本地实时显示，不会被录制、存储或上传。

内购买

应用内购买通过 Apple App Store 处理，购买信息由 Apple 管理，我们不存储任何支付信息。

第三方服务

AosomeBoard 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪服务。

儿童隐私

本应用适合所有年龄段使用（4+），不收集任何用户数据，对儿童隐私保护完全符合相关法规。

AosomeBoard Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

AosomeBoard respects and protects user privacy. This app does not collect, upload, or share any personal user data.

Data Collection

AosomeBoard does not collect any user data, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Usage behavior data
  • Crash reports
  • Analytics data

Local Storage

The app only stores the following information locally on your device:

  • App settings (stored via UserDefaults)
  • Trial period start date
  • Recently opened file bookmarks (Security-Scoped Bookmarks)
  • Recently used colors

This data is used solely for app functionality and is never transmitted to any server.

Camera Access

The app accesses the camera only when you actively enable the "Camera Picture-in-Picture" feature. The camera feed is only displayed locally in real time and is never recorded, stored, or uploaded.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through the Apple App Store. Purchase information is managed by Apple. We do not store any payment information.

Third-Party Services

AosomeBoard does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages (4+). It does not collect any user data and fully complies with children's privacy regulations.
